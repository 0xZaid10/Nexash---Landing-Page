HOW IT WORKS

From message to compliant payment in four steps.

01

Get Your Wallet

Send /start to the Nexash bot. A wallet is created instantly and tied to your Telegram identity. No setup, no seed phrases, no browser extension.

02

Get Verified

Run /kyc. Nexash issues a KYC attestation signed by our registered issuer key and anchors it permanently on HashKey Chain mainnet. Your compliance status lives on-chain, forever.

03

Fund Your Wallet

Run /faucet. Your wallet is funded with testnet USDC automatically. Check your balance anytime with /wallet.

04

Send a Compliant Payment

Run /pay [amount] [address]. Nexash checks your on-chain KYC, builds a compliant HSP mandate, executes the on-chain transfer, and returns a cryptographic ACCEPT verdict.

FEATURES

Everything you need.

On-Chain Compliance

KYC attestations anchored permanently on HashKey Chain mainnet. Our smart contract enforces compliance before any payment — not advisory, not optional, enforced at the contract level.

HSP Compliant Payments

Every payment is a signed HSP mandate with a KYC attestation. The Coordinator independently verifies both. You get a cryptographic ACCEPT verdict and an Explorer link for every transaction.

AI Payroll Agent

Describe payments in plain English. The AI agent parses instructions, resolves payee addresses, and flags anomalies before anything moves. "Send alice 500 USDC and hold bob's until his KYC renews" — done.

Live Market Intelligence

Real-time data from HashKey Exchange with RSI, EMA (9/21/50), VWAP, and ATR across three timeframes. The AI trading agent gives a specific multi-timeframe view with confidence rating.

Paper Trading

Simulate trades with live HashKey Exchange prices. Track your portfolio with real-time P&L, position history, and realized gains. Live trading coming soon, In Sha Allah.

Telegram-Native Wallets

Every user gets a wallet the moment they message the bot. Works on any phone, any country, any time. The most accessible onboarding in crypto.

The Foundation Mainnet Deployed

HashKey Chain

Both Nexash smart contracts are deployed on HashKey Chain mainnet (chainId 177). All compliance state — KYC attestations, issuer registrations, policy enforcement — lives on HashKey Chain permanently.

The Payment Rail Registered Issuer

HashKey Settlement Protocol

Nexash is a registered trusted compliance issuer on HSP. Every payment is submitted as an HSP v1 mandate with a signed attestation. The Coordinator independently verifies settlement and compliance. Nexash also runs its own independent HSP verifier on every payment.

The Market Data Source Live Data

HashKey Exchange

All market data, prices, and trading signals come directly from HashKey Exchange's public API. RSI, EMA, VWAP, ATR, and multi-timeframe candles are computed from live HashKey Exchange OHLCV data.

The Identity Layer In Development

NexaID

A zkTLS-based identity network on HashKey Chain that proves real-world KYC without exposing personal data. Nexash uses NexaID as the evidence source for production KYC attestations. Integration in development.
